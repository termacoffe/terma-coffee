Welcome to Terma Coffee

Welcome to Terma Coffee — where every cup tells a story of Nepal's finest highlands. Nestled in the heart of Balaju‑16, Kathmandu, Terma Coffee is not just a coffee shop; it is a celebration of Nepali coffee culture, craftsmanship, and community.

At Terma Coffee, we believe that great coffee should never come at a great price. That is why we are proud to offer A‑grade quality coffee at the most affordable prices in Kathmandu.

Our coffee is fully grown and carefully crafted in Nuwakot, one of Nepal's most fertile regions. From the lush green hills of Nuwakot, our beans are cultivated with love, harvested at peak ripeness, and processed with traditional care to preserve every note of flavor.

We are a proudly local Nepali brand — from the farm to your cup, every step happens right here in Nepal. Terma Coffee was founded by Sugam Pudasaini, with a mission to put Nepali coffee on the map.
